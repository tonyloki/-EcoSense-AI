Sustainability Policy Knowledge Base

1. Energy Efficiency Standards
   - LED lighting reduces consumption by 75%
   - Smart thermostats save 10-15% on HVAC costs
   - Building automation systems optimize peak demand

2. Water Conservation Guidelines
   - Low-flow fixtures reduce usage by 30%
   - Rainwater harvesting systems for irrigation
   - Wastewater recycling for non-potable uses

3. Carbon Footprint Reduction
   - Renewable energy sources: solar, wind, geothermal
   - Electric vehicle adoption for transport
   - Supply chain optimization strategies

4. Compliance and Certifications
   - ISO 50001: Energy Management Systems
   - LEED Certification levels
   - Carbon Neutral certifications

5. Best Practices
   - Conduct energy audits quarterly
   - Implement employee sustainability programs
   - Monitor and report metrics monthly
